SYMPOSIUM ON CYBER KEY SECURITY AND DATA PRIVACY

Feb. 26, 2025 - EMC 3 - JTA Building, Holy Trinity College (DICT R12 - SAR. PROV & GENSAN)

I was given the chance to be part of one of the organizers of the Symposium on Cybersecurity and Data Privacy held by DICT Sarangani–GenSan last February 26, 2025, at EMC 3, JTA Building, Holy Trinity College. First- and second-year CETE students attended the event for a very informative discussion on internet safety, which featured topics on the E-Gov app, RA 10173 (Data Privacy Act), and RA 10175 (Cybercrime Prevention Act).

As a member of the organizing team, I facilitated the smooth operation of the program and assisted in spreading the awareness of cybersecurity to participants.

The event focused on responsible internet use, best practices for data protection, and being aware in today's digital age—reminding everyone to "Think Before You Click" for a safer internet experience.
