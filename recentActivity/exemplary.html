LGU-GENSAN OJT CULMINATION (EXEMPLARY AWARD)

June 25, 2025 - Venue 88, General Santos City
Image Source: https://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=1535945961868401&set=pb.100063590364810.-2207520000&type=3

On June 25, 2025, during our OJT Culmination held at Venue 88, I was honored to receive an Exemplary Award from the Local Government Unit of General Santos (LGU-Gensan). Out of over 300 OJT interns, less than 20 of us were recognized—and I was one of them.

This award was given in recognition of my contribution to the creation of PPETS (Property and Procurement Electronic Tracking System), which was formerly known as ADTS (Asset Distribution and Tracking System)—a digital solution I developed to streamline workflows and improve the efficiency of internal asset tracking within the LGU-City Accountants Office.

This achievement was the result of countless trials, revisions, and learning moments. As a student stepping into a real government workspace, I was challenged to think beyond theory and deliver something that could create actual impact. This recognition reflects not just the output, but the growth and resilience I built along the way.

It’s an experience I will carry forward—proof that with the right mindset and willingness to learn, even interns can make a meaningful difference.
