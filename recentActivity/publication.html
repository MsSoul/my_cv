RESEARCH PUBLICATION: AMERICAN JOURNAL OF SMART TECHNOLOGY AND SOLUTIONS (AJSTS)

Published in Volume 4, No. 2 (2025) - International Peer-Reviewed Journal
Image Source: https://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=1535945961868401&set=pb.100063590364810.-2207520000&type=3

I am deeply honored to share the successful international publication of our research paper titled “Optimizing Screen Time Management for Children’s Devices: Leveraging Token Bucket and Time-Based Algorithms in a Famie Parental Control System.” This study was completed alongside my co-authors, Regine Karla J. Panilaga and Alic Jhos L. Magallamento, following our graduation with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science in June 2025.

Our work has been officially featured in Volume 4, No. 2 (2025) of the American Journal of Smart Technology and Solutions (AJSTS), an open-access and double-blind peer-reviewed international journal devoted to advancements in smart technologies, innovation, and automation. This study contributes algorithmic data points and frameworks intended to advance modern digital parenting and device enforcement solutions.

We extend our sincerest gratitude to our dedicated thesis advisers, Prof. Abejah S. Paculdo and Prof. Charisse S. Ronquillo, for their unwavering guidance. We also gratefully recognize the invaluable structural guidance of our Research Director, Dr. Haya Jane E. Elan, and the precise statistical expertise of Sir Hardy M. Bankas, whose collective mentorship heavily shaped the academic rigor and final impact of this paper.
