SOCI X CETE DONATION TO ALABEL NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL

Aug. 16, 2024 - Alabel National High School, Alabel, Sarangani Province

On August 16, 2024, I had the honor of participating in the SOCI x CETE donation event at Alabel National High School, Sarangani Province, where our department, the College of Engineering and Technology Education (CETE), in collaboration with SOCI, donated five new computer sets to support the school’s learning resources.

Being selected to assist in bringing this kind contribution was indeed humbling and uplifting, as it paved the way for learners to engage with new technology and better their learning experience.

This activity not only showcased our dedication to community service but also reaffirmed the value of closing learning gaps through cooperation and innovation.
