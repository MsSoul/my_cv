CETE OJT CULMINATION (MOST OUTSTANDING OJT BSCS AWARD)

June 5, 2025 - Grand Summit Hotel, General Santos City
Image Source: https://www.facebook.com/share/p/1FBgon14qD/

Last June 5, 2025, the College of Engineering and Technology Education (CETE) held our OJT Culmination at the Grand Summit Hotel here in General Santos City. It was a formal gala dedicated to celebrating our hard work and marking our official transition from the classroom into the corporate tech world alongside my fellow IT, Computer Science, and Computer Engineering peers.

During the awards segment, I was incredibly proud and deeply humbled to receive the highest distinction for our program—the Most Outstanding OJT Award for Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS). This award is given to the intern who demonstrates peak technical performance and strong professional behavior in a real-world industry environment.
