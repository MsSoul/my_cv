EARTHQUAKE DRILL AT LGU-GENSAN - CITY ACCOUNTANT’S OFFICE

March 13, 2025 - City Accountant’s Office, City Hall

On March 13, 2025, I actively participated in the Earthquake Drill conducted at the City Accountant’s Office, City Hall, General Santos City. The drill was organized to enhance preparedness and ensure the safety of all employees in the event of an earthquake.

During the exercise, I followed proper safety protocols such as “Drop, Cover, and Hold On,” and participated in the orderly evacuation to designated safe zones. This practical experience reinforced the importance of being vigilant and well-prepared for natural disasters, helping to build a culture of safety and quick response within the workplace.

The drill not only heightened my awareness of emergency procedures but also strengthened our team’s ability to work together calmly and efficiently under pressure.
