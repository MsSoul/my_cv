HTC 40TH FOUNDATION ANNIVERSARY COMMUNITY OUTREACH DAY

Nov. 16, 2024 - Purok Sarif Mucsin, Brgy. Baluan, General Santos City

On November 16, 2024, I took part in the HTC 40th Foundation Anniversary Community Day as a representative of the College of Engineering and Technology Education (CETE). The outreach activity was held at Purok Shariff Mucsins, Brgy. Baluan, General Santos City, in collaboration with HTC-SSC, CBMA, CCJE, TESDA, and The Quill Publication.

As part of this community engagement, I helped facilitate and support seminars such as the Basic Hygiene Seminar for children and the Disaster Risk Reduction and Management (DRRM) Seminar for members of the Fisher Folks Association.

This meaningful experience deepened my appreciation for civic involvement and highlighted the importance of using education and teamwork to positively impact others.
