3RD GENERAL SANTOS CITY NATIONAL STATISTICS MONTH RESEARCH FORUM

Oct. 3, 2025 - Notre Dame of Dadiangas University (NDDU), General Santos City
Image Source: https://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=1535945961868401&set=pb.100063590364810.-2207520000&type=3

Grateful and honored to be one of the Top 10 Research Paper Finalists out of 95 participants at the 3rd General Santos City National Statistics Month Research Forum last October 3, 2025, at NDDU Gensan. I am very thankful for the opportunity to represent our school and share our research study.

Special thanks to my co-author, Alicjhos Magallamento, and to Regine Panilaga, who was with me throughout the competition. Thank you, Reg, for all your effort—especially for creating the PPT I presented. Your dedication and sleepless nights truly paid off, and it was even praised by the judges.

To our advisers, Prof. Abejah Paculdo, Abdreiyll Ge, and Prof. Charisse Ronquillo, and to Ma’am Haya, our Research Director, thank you for the endless support and guidance.

The title may not have been ours, but being among the 4 student finalists who presented alongside professionals, government workers, and doctorate holders is already a huge achievement. Truly a humbling and memorable experience.
