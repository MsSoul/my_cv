LABAN GIRL POWER - KAPIHAN NI JUANA

March 21, 2024 - Veranza Mall, General Santos City

I participated in the Women's Month event titled “Laban Girl Power: Kapehan ni Juana” held at Veranza Mall, General Santos City, on March 21, 2024. The event served as a meaningful platform to celebrate the strength, resilience, and contributions of women in various sectors of society.

Through open discussions, inspiring stories, and community engagement, the activity empowered participants to advocate for gender equality and women's rights.

Being part of this celebration allowed me to reflect on the importance of women’s roles in nation-building and deepened my commitment to supporting initiatives that uplift and empower women in all aspects of life.
