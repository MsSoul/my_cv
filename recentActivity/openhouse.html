HTC OPEN HOUSE 2024

Nov. 21-29, 2024 - Holy Trinity College

During the HTC Open House 2024, held from November 21 to 29, 2024 at HTC, I participated as a representative of our department by showcasing two of my programming projects: the CETE Quiz Game, a web-based quiz game focused on web design concepts, and a Tic Tac Toe mobile app game.

Throughout the event, I presented these interactive games to students, teachers, and visitors, explaining the development process, design principles, and the technologies used behind each project. I discussed the core logic, user interface design, and the programming languages and frameworks applied—such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Flutter.

This opportunity allowed me to demonstrate not only my technical skills but also my ability to communicate and explain complex coding concepts to a broader audience.
