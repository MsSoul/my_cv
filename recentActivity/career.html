CAREER AWARENESS DAY

March 12, 2024 - Veranza Activity Center, General Santos City

I participated in the Career Awareness Day held on March 12, 2024, at the Veranza Activity Center, Veranza Drive, General Santos City. The event was organized by the IT and Business Process Association of the Philippines (IBPAP), in partnership with the Department of Information and Communications Technology (DICT) and the Department of Trade and Industry (DTI).

This participation demonstrates my active engagement in exploring potential career paths and opportunities, as well as gaining valuable insights into the IT-BPM industry and other emerging fields.

It was a meaningful experience that broadened my understanding of workforce demands and helped me align my skills and interests with the needs of today’s job market.
