Leadership & Service Details

DEPARTMENT PUBLIC INFORMATION OFFICER (PIO) — COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING & TECHNOLOGY EDUCATION

Service Period: School Year 2024 - 2025 | Awarded: June 20, 2025

I am deeply honored to have been awarded this Certificate of Appreciation in recognition of my contributions and valuable leadership role as the PIO (Public Information Officer) of the College of Engineering and Technology Education Department at Holy Trinity College of General Santos City for the School Year 2024–2025.

Serving in this capacity, I was responsible for streamlining official communications, managing information dissemination, and serving as the key liaison between the student body, departmental organizations, and the college faculty. This administrative role required exceptional communication skills, media management, timely problem-solving, and the coordination of public relations for all academic and extra-curricular departmental events.

The distinction was officially given at the Holy Trinity College campus on Fiscal Daproza Avenue, General Santos City. The certificate is signed by Charisse S. Ronquillo, MIT (Dean, College of Engineering and Technology Education), Joana Gene C. Patling, LPT (Student Affairs and Development Officer), and Engr. Lloyd O. Arenas, REE, RME (Adviser). This milestone reflects my continuing progression in student leadership, project coordination, and structural communications management alongside my technical competencies.
