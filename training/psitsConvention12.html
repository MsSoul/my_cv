12TH PSITS REGIONAL CONVENTION

May 3, 2024 - May 5, 2024 | MARBEL, SOUTH COTABATO

From May 3 to 5, 2024, I joined and helped facilitate the 12th PSITS Regional Convention in Marbel, South Cotabato. As both a participant and a member of the support team, I contributed to the smooth execution of the event by assisting with various tasks such as distributing food, checking in students at our accommodation hotel, and supporting our teachers during the competition schedules of other participants.

Additionally, I helped prepare our performers by doing makeup for our singers. This experience allowed me to develop my organizational, creative, and teamwork skills while actively engaging with peers from different schools in the region.
