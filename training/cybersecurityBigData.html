ICT TRENDS IN CYBERSECURITY AND BIG DATA ANALYTICS

June 7, 2024 | DICT R-XII VIA ZOOM

I actively participated in the webinar entitled "ICT Trends in Cybersecurity and Big Data Analytics", held on June 7, 2024, as part of the National ICT Month celebration themed "Bayang Digital ang Bagong Pilipinas." This 3-hour webinar, delivered via the Zoom platform, provided valuable insights into current trends within the ICT sector, specifically focusing on Cybersecurity and Big Data Analytics.

The event featured expert speakers who discussed emerging threats in cybersecurity, innovative strategies for data protection, and the growing importance of big data in decision-making processes across various industries. Through interactive presentations and case studies, I gained a deeper understanding of how organizations are leveraging advanced analytics and security measures to address complex challenges in the digital age.

This webinar enhanced my awareness of the evolving ICT landscape and reinforced the critical role of cybersecurity and data analytics skills in my ongoing academic and professional development.
