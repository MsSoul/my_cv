Seminar Details

Cybersecurity Awareness on National Women's Month 2025

March 20, 2025 | DICT XII via Zoom

Participating in the Cybersecurity Awareness Webinar hosted by DICT Region XII via Zoom on March 20, 2025, in celebration of National Women’s Month with the theme “Babae sa Lahat ng Sektor, Aangat ang Bukas sa Bagong Pilipinas,” was both meaningful and empowering.

The session emphasized the importance of safe and responsible online behavior for women and the broader community, featuring expert talks and real-life case studies on common cyber threats such as phishing, online scams, data breaches, and social media risks. I gained practical knowledge on protecting personal data, upholding digital privacy, and promoting cybersecurity awareness in my surroundings.

This experience not only deepened my understanding of online safety but also reinforced the need to educate and empower women in navigating the digital space. My participation was formally recognized with a certificate of attendance issued by DICT Region XII (SOCCSKSARGEN and LaMa).
