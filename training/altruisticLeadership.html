LEADERSHIP SEMINAR-WORKSHOP

October 20, 2023 | EMC 2 - HTC

I participated in the Leadership Seminar-Workshop held at the Educational Media Center 2, Holy Trinity College of General Santos City, on October 20, 2023. The seminar-workshop, themed “We Before Me: Keeping the Flame of Altruistic Leadership,” brought together student leaders to reflect on the value of selfless service and collaborative leadership.

This participation demonstrates my active engagement in activities focused on developing leadership skills, fostering a strong sense of responsibility, and understanding the core principles of altruistic leadership.

The experience deepened my appreciation for servant leadership and inspired me to lead with empathy, purpose, and integrity.
