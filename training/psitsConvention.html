Seminar Details

13th PSITS Regional Convention 2024

April 11, 2025 - April 12, 2025 | SEAIT, TUPI, SOUTH COTABATO

I was part of helping during the 13th PSITS Regional Convention 2024 on April 11–12, 2025, at SEAIT, Tupi, South Cotabato. I helped my school delegation with different non-academic activities, more specifically by assisting in preparing contestants for the singing trio, hip-hop dance and bench yelling competitions.

I helped with makeup and presentation overall, making sure our performers were poised and ready for the stage. Working on the behind-the-scenes crew enabled me to serve the success of our school in an inspiring and creative manner.

Through this experience, my teamwork, school spirit, and ability to adapt were solidified, while my organizational and interpersonal skills were further enhanced in an active and fast-paced setting.
