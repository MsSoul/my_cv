LGU GENSAN - CITY ACCOUNTANT'S OFFICE

Feb. 04, 2025 - March 28, 2025

As part of my on-the-job training at the City Accountant's Office, I designed the mobile application for the Asset Distribution and Tracking System (ADTS), a full-stack solution developed using Flutter, Node.js, and MySQL to automate asset lending, borrowing, returning, and transfer processes. While my fellow trainee handled the web platform, I focused on building a secure and user-friendly mobile interface, incorporating OTP authentication, real-time notifications, and interactive dashboards.

Through independent debugging, and efficient resource utilization, I enhanced the app's functionality and reliability. This experience significantly improved my technical skills in mobile development and backend integration, while also strengthening my collaboration, problem-solving, and adaptability.

Although only 162 hours were required, I voluntarily extended my internship to 256 hours to maximize learning and contribute more meaningfully. Additionally, I helped boost team morale and well-being by organizing and leading daily Zumba sessions as part of our workplace routine.
