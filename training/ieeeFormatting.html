STRUCTURING AND FORMATTING IEEE PAPERS: ESSENTIAL GUIDELINES

July 10, 2024 - July 18, 2024 | HTC

I successfully completed the 30-hour workshop on "Structuring and Formatting IEEE Papers: Essential Guidelines" held at Holy Trinity College of General Santos City from July 10, 2024, to July 18, 2024.

This workshop, as evidenced by the Certificate of Completion, provided me with valuable knowledge and practical skills in adhering to IEEE standards for paper preparation.

The training, conducted at COMLAB 2 of the college, was led by the trainer, Engr. Lloyd O. Arenas, REE, RME.
