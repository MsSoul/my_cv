Recognition Details

EXEMPLARY AWARDEE — LGU GENSAN HR ACADEMY

Given this 25th day of June 2025

During the official culmination ceremony held by the Local Government Unit of General Santos City and the Human Resource Management and Development Office (HRMDO) HR Academy, I was presented with two milestone honors: my official Certificate of Completion for rendering a total of Two Hundred Sixty (260) On-The-Job Training hours, alongside the highly selective Exemplary Awardee distinction.

My internship at the City Accountant's Office ran from February 4, 2025, up to March 26, 2025. During this period, I was tasked with the development of the full-stack mobile application for the PPETS (Property and Procurement Electronic Tracking System), which was formerly known as ADTS (Asset Distribution and Tracking System). The system was custom-engineered as a full-stack solution to automate asset lending, borrowing, returning, control management, and logistical auditing for local government property records.

Both honors were officially attested by Acting City HRMD Officer Atty. Rosendo A. Roque and signed by City Mayor Hon. Lorelie Geronimo Pacquiao. This combined recognition serves as a testament to my dedication to technical efficiency, full-stack systems integration, and leveraging software engineering to modernize administrative public sector workflows.
