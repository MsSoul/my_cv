STARTUP TALK Series 2025 – Session 3!

February 27, 2025 | DICT XII via GMEET

I participated in the Startup Talk Series 2025 – Session 3, held on February 27, 2025, via Google Meet and organized by DICT Region XII. The session featured experienced entrepreneurs and industry experts who shared their journeys, insights, and practical advice on starting and sustaining a successful business.

Topics included ideation, funding, digital tools for startups, and overcoming challenges in today’s competitive market. The talk was both inspiring and informative, fueling my interest in entrepreneurship and providing me with a clearer understanding of the startup ecosystem.

It was a valuable learning opportunity that encouraged innovation, resilience, and the pursuit of impactful ideas in the digital economy.
