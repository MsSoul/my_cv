Research & Recognition Details

TOP 10 RESEARCH PAPER FINALIST — 3RD GENSAN NATIONAL STATISTICS MONTH RESEARCH FORUM

Presented: October 3, 2025 | Notre Dame of Dadiangas University

Our study "Optimizing Screen Time Management For Children's Devices: Leveraging Token Bucket And Time-Based Algorithms In A Famie Parental Control System" was recognized as one of the Top 10 Research Paper Finalists out of 95 competitive abstract submissions at the 3rd General Santos City National Statistics Month Research Forum held at Notre Dame of Dadiangas University (NDDU). This forum was jointly organized by the Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) Sarangani Provincial Statistical Office and the Local Government Unit of General Santos City under the theme “Accelerating the SDG Pace of Progress: Mobilizing Data for All”.

Standing as one of only four student finalists selected to present on this public stage, I competed directly alongside seasoned industry professionals, government researchers, and doctorate holders. This milestone highlighted the academic rigor, competitive standard, and viability of our research study within the regional academic community.

The certificate was officially presented and signed by Ismael B. Ramos, Jr. (Chief Statistical Specialist, PSA Sarangani) and Judith C. Janiola, MPA (City Government Department Head II, City Population Management Office). This achievement underscores my proficiency in data-driven research methodologies, statistical analysis, and my commitment to contributing to conversations surrounding sustainable development goals (SDGs) through data mobilization.
