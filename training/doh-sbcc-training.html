Training & Seminar Details

INFODEMIC RESPONSE THROUGH TARGETED SBCC MATERIALS

Held: September 11, 2025 | Holy Trinity College, General Santos City

I am honored to have attended the training program titled "Infodemic Response through the Creation of Targeted Social and Behavior Change Communication (SBCC) Materials," hosted by the Department of Health (DOH), Center for Health Development SOCCSKSARGEN Region.

The training session, conducted on September 11, 2025, at Holy Trinity College, provided an intensive look into the mechanisms of public health communication. It focused on the strategic creation of SBCC materials designed to combat misinformation—or "infodemics"—by ensuring that health information is accurate, accessible, and behaviorally impactful for diverse community audiences.

This certificate was officially issued and signed by Aristides Concepcion Tan, MD, MPH, CESO III (Director IV). Participating in this program enhanced my understanding of how technical communication and strategic messaging serve as critical tools in public health initiatives, further broadening my interdisciplinary skill set beyond engineering and IT.
