Seminar Details

ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE FOR EDUCATION AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP

June 13, 2024 | DICT VIA ZOOM

I actively participated in the webinar titled "Artificial Intelligence for Education and Entrepreneurship," held on June 13, 2024, as part of the National ICT Month celebration with the theme "Bayang Digital ang Bagong Pilipinas." The 3-hour virtual event, conducted via Zoom, provided valuable insights into the applications of AI in education and business.

The certificate, digitally signed by Engr. Ibrahim B. Guiapar Jr., Regional Director of DICT Region XII (SOCCSKSARGEN), acknowledges my engagement and contribution to the session, reflecting my commitment to embracing digital transformation for a progressive Philippines.
