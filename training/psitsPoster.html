Training & Seminar Details

13TH PSITS REGIONAL CONVENTION 2024

SEAIT, Tupi, South Cotabato

I had the privilege of representing my school at the 13th PSITS Regional Convention 2024 held at SEAIT, Tupi, South Cotabato, where I participated in the Research Poster Presentation Competition alongside 25 institutions from Region XII.

I showcased my thesis study, the Famie Parental Control System, which features a Time-Based Algorithm and a Token Bucket Algorithm for efficient screen time management. My research poster was designed to effectively communicate the study's objectives, methodologies, and key findings in an engaging and visually appealing manner.

Through careful planning, thoughtful design, and clear communication, I successfully captured the attention of both the judges and the audience, ultimately winning first place in the competition.
