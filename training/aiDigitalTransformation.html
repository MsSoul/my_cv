AI FOR DIGITAL TRANSFORMATION (INCLUDES GENAI)

June 25, 2024 | WGDT VIA ZOOM

I had the opportunity to participate in the “AI for Digital Transformation (includes Gen AI)” webinar conducted via Zoom by the Wadhwani Center for Government Digital Transformation, a global foundation, in partnership with the Department of Information and Communications Technology (DICT) Region XII.

Held on June 25, 2024, the session provided valuable insights into how Artificial Intelligence, including Generative AI, can be leveraged to drive innovation and efficiency in digital governance and public service. Through this training, I gained a deeper understanding of AI-driven solutions and their practical applications in transforming organizational operations and service delivery.

Successfully completing the course has enhanced my knowledge and skills in digital transformation, preparing me to contribute more effectively to tech-driven initiatives in the public sector.
