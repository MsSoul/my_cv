HTC LEADERSHIP SEMINAR-WORKSHOP

October 11, 2024 | HTC EMC 3

Joining the seminar-workshop "Gear Up! Ready, Set, Lead!: Equipping Genuine, Empowered, Altruistic, Resilient Trinitarian Leaders" held at Holy Trinity College of General Santos City on October 11, 2024, equipped me with valuable lessons and hands-on knowledge to better become a leader.

The experience, attested to by my Certificate of Participation, provided an interactive arena to learn and personify the characteristics of authentic, empowered, altruistic, and resilient leadership in the Trinitarian fellowship.

It enriched my appreciation for purpose-driven leadership and how to contribute positively and serve my community with commitment and integrity.
