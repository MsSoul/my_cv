SAFER INTERNET DAY 2025

February 27, 2025 | DICT XII VIA ZOOM

Having successfully participated in the Cybersecurity Awareness Webinar held via Zoom on February 27, 2025, in celebration of Safer Internet Day 2025, I gained valuable insights into promoting online safety, particularly for the youth.

The webinar, organized by the Department of Information and Communications Technology (DICT) Region XII (SOCCSKSARGEN and LaMa), under the theme "Digital Bayanihan, Para sa Kabataang Ligtas at Protektado Online," equipped me with essential knowledge and strategies to contribute to a safer digital environment.
