ADVANCED DATABASE MANAGEMENT SYSTEMS

July 1, 2024 - July 9, 2024 | HTC

I successfully completed the 30-hour workshop on "Advanced Database Management Systems" held at Holy Trinity College of General Santos City from July 1 to July 9, 2024. The training took place at COMLAB 2 and was facilitated by Prof. Abejah S. Paculdo, MIT.

This workshop provided comprehensive knowledge and practical skills in handling complex database systems, covering topics such as query optimization, indexing, transaction management, and database security.

The hands-on activities enhanced my technical competencies and deepened my understanding of database administration in real-world scenarios. Although I do not have a formal certificate, I have documented proof of my active participation through photos taken during the workshop.
