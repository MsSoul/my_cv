Leadership & Service Details

DEPARTMENT TREASURER — COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING & TECHNOLOGY EDUCATION

Service Period: School Year 2023 - 2024 | Awarded: June 3, 2024

I am deeply honored to have been awarded this Certificate of Appreciation in recognition of my contributions and valuable leadership role as the Treasurer of the College of Engineering and Technology Education Department at Holy Trinity College of General Santos City for the School Year 2023–2024.

In this position, I managed financial oversight, budget tracking, and resource administration for departmental student projects, academic activities, and organization initiatives. This administrative background demanded a high level of accountability, precise spreadsheet organization, financial management, and direct communication with both the student body and executive faculty members.

The distinction was officially given at the Holy Trinity College campus on Fiscal Daproza Avenue, General Santos City. The certificate is signed by Charisse S. Ronquillo, MIT (Dean, College of Engineering and Technology Education), Joana Gene C. Patling, LPT (Student Affairs and Development Officer), and Engr. Lloyd O. Arenas, REE, RME (Adviser). This experience highlights my foundational skills in project coordination, institutional leadership, and operational management alongside my technical academic background.
