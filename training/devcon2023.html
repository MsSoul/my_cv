DEVELOPMENT CONFERENCE (DEVCON) 2023

May 12, 2023 | KCC CONVENTION

I had the opportunity to participate in DEVCON 2023 (Development Conference) held on May 12, 2023, at the KCC Convention Center, which brought together students and professionals from various schools and institutions.

It was a whole-day event filled with insightful discussions and talks from seven different speakers, each sharing valuable knowledge on innovation, technology trends, and career growth in the IT industry.

Engaging with fellow participants and industry experts not only broadened my understanding of the tech landscape but also inspired me to further develop my skills and aspirations in the field of information technology.
