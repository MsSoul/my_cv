11TH PSITS REGIONAL CONVENTION 2023

April 28, 2023 | KCC CONVENTION

On April 28, 2023, I proudly represented our institution as a Quiz Bowl participant during the 11th PSITS Regional Convention 2023 held at the KCC Convention Center. It was my first-ever quiz bowl experience, yet I never hesitated to step out of my comfort zone because I didn’t want to limit myself or my potential.

Competing alongside students from various schools in Region XII, the event tested our technical knowledge, critical thinking, and composure under pressure.

Despite being new to such competitions, I embraced the challenge with determination and pride, making it a memorable and empowering milestone in my academic journey.
