INTRODUCTION TO DATA ANALYTICS

July 10, 2024 - July 18, 2024 | HTC

Another 30-hour workshop I successfully completed as part of my preparation for our upcoming thesis defense was the "Introduction to Data Analytics," held at Holy Trinity College of General Santos City from July 10 to July 18, 2024, and conducted by Prof. Hardy Bankas.

The training, conducted at COMLAB 2, equipped me with essential knowledge in data collection, preprocessing, visualization, and introductory statistical analysis. It provided a solid foundation in understanding and interpreting data—an essential skill for research and data-driven analysis.

Although I do not have a certificate, I have photos that serve as proof of my participation and engagement throughout the workshop, reflecting my commitment to strengthening my skills as an IT student.
