EARTHQUAKE DRILL at LGU-GENSAN- CITY ACCOUNTANT’S OFFICE
(MARCH 13,2025 -CITY ACCOUNTANT’S OFFICE- CITY HALL)
On March 13, 2025, I actively participated in the Earthquake Drill conducted at the City Accountant’s Office, City Hall, General Santos City. The drill was organized to enhance preparedness and ensure the safety of all employees in the event of an earthquake. During the exercise, I followed proper safety protocols such as “Drop, Cover, and Hold On,” and participated in the orderly evacuation to designated safe zones. This practical experience reinforced the importance of being vigilant and well-prepared for natural disasters, helping to build a culture of safety and quick response within the workplace. The drill not only heightened my awareness of emergency procedures but also strengthened our team’s ability to work together calmly and efficiently under pressure.
SYMPOSIUM ON CYBER SECURITY AND DATA PRIVACY seminar conducted by DICT R12 - SAR. PROV (& GENSAN)
(FEB. 26, 2025 - EMC 3 - JTA BUILDING HTC)
I was given the chance to be part of one of the organizers of the Symposium on Cybersecurity and Data Privacy held by DICT Sarangani–GenSan last February 26, 2025, at EMC 3, JTA Building, Holy Trinity College. First- and second-year CETE students attended the event for a very informative discussion on internet safety, which featured topics on the E-Gov app, RA 10173 (Data Privacy Act), and RA 10175 (Cybercrime Prevention Act). As a member of the organizing team, I facilitated the smooth operation of the program and assisted in spreading the awareness of cybersecurity to participants. The event focused on responsible internet use, best practices for data protection, and being aware in today's digital age—reminding everyone to "Think Before You Click" for a safer internet experience.
